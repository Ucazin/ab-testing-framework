A/B Test Pre-Experiment Design
==============================

Hypothesis
    The new checkout-button variant will lift conversion-to-purchase by
    at least +8% (relative) over baseline.

Inputs
    Baseline conversion rate (control)   :  5.00%
    Smallest detectable relative lift    :  +8%
    Implied treatment conversion rate    :  0.0540
    Significance level (alpha, 2-sided)  :  0.05
    Statistical power (1 - beta)         :  0.8

Required sample size
    Per arm                              :  48,348 visitors
    Total                                :  96,696 visitors

Traffic and duration
    Eligible visitors / day              :  12,000
    Required days to fully power test    :  8.1

Guardrail metrics (must not regress)
    - Average revenue per visitor (ARPV)
    - Page-load time
    - Cart-abandonment rate (anything that signals confusion from the new variant)

Stopping rules
    - Hard stop at day 9 regardless of significance.
    - No peeking before day 4.
    - SRM check at day 2: if chi-square p < 0.01, pause and investigate.
